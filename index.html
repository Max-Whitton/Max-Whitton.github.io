About

I’m a Ph.D. student in Computer Science at Boston University, where I research deep learning and vision–language models with a focus on data and pretraining. Last May, I graduated from Cornell University with a degree in Computer Science, and worked in industry building AI-driven tools deployed across a global engineering organization. I enjoy working at the intersection of research and real-world impact, from multimodal AI systems to large-scale data and model deployment.
